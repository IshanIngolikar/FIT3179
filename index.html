Healthcare Analytics Dashboard

Healthcare Facility Map

This map shows the distribution of healthcare facilities across Australia. Use the dropdown to zoom in on a specific state. Trends in facility location can help identify regions with greater healthcare infrastructure.

Bed Availability Per Person

This chart illustrates the bed availability per person in each state. States with higher bed availability typically have better-equipped healthcare systems to handle patient influx during critical times, such as pandemics.

Sectoral Analysis: Public vs Private Hospitals

This pie chart shows the distribution of public and private hospitals across states. Select a specific state to analyze the sectoral distribution. States with more public hospitals may focus more on universal healthcare.

Expenditure Breakdown

This treemap represents healthcare expenditure across various sectors. Larger blocks indicate higher expenditure in a given sector, which provides insight into how healthcare funding is allocated across different areas.

Government Expenditure Over Time

This line chart shows how government healthcare expenditure has evolved over time, broken down by state. Identify trends and patterns, such as periods of increased spending, which may align with public health crises or policy changes.
